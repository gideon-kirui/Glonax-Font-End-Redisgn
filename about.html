Our Core Values

These values are not just words on a wall — they are the operating principles behind every decision, service, and relationship at Glonax.

01

Excellence in Every Detail

We hold ourselves to the highest standard in everything — from how we answer the phone to how we close a property deal. No detail is too small, no client too minor.

02

Integrity Without Compromise

Trust is our currency. We operate with full transparency in all transactions, communications, and partnerships — standing firm in our ethics even when it costs us.

03

Innovation & Adaptability

Markets evolve. We evolve with them. Our teams continuously adopt new tools, methodologies, and approaches to stay ahead of industry shifts and client expectations.

04

Community Impact

We are deeply rooted in Nakuru and the broader Kenyan community. Our success is measured not just by revenue, but by the livelihoods we improve and the opportunities we create.

05

Client Confidentiality

Your information, your property, your plans — we treat them with the same discretion we would want for ourselves. Our clients' privacy is sacred.

06

Results-Driven Partnership

We don't just deliver services — we deliver outcomes. Every engagement begins with understanding your goals and ends when those goals are measurably achieved.

Our Journey

The Glonax Story

18
2018

The Beginning — Commercial Agencies

Founded as Glonax Commercial Agencies Limited, we started with a singular focus: making property ownership and management accessible, transparent, and trustworthy in Nakuru.

20
2020

Expanding Into Creative Media

Recognizing the growing demand for quality content and branding in Kenya, we launched our Creative Media division — bringing storytelling and digital excellence to local and regional clients.

22
2022

Hospitality Academy Launch

We opened the Premium International Hospitality Academy, creating a structured pathway for Kenyan youth to access world-class hospitality training and international placement opportunities.

25
2025

Holdings — One Roof, Three Divisions

Rebranded as Glonax Holdings Limited, unifying our three divisions under one powerful umbrella with a shared vision, shared values, and shared commitment to excellence.

Glonax Holdings Limited was born from a simple but powerful belief: that Kenyans deserve access to professional, trustworthy, and world-class services — not just in Nairobi, but in every corner of the country.

What started as a boutique real estate firm in Nakuru has grown into one of the region's most distinctive multi-division holding companies — with deep expertise in property, creative media, and hospitality training.

"We don't just serve clients — we build futures. Every property transaction, every brand we build, every professional we train carries the Glonax promise of excellence."
— Glonax Holdings Leadership

Today, from our offices at Gate House Nakuru — 5th Floor, Suite 506 — we serve hundreds of clients across Kenya, with a team of certified professionals who share one non-negotiable standard: excellence.
